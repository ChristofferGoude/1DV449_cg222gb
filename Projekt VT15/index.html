Welcome to Hakkiko! Hakkiko is a website where you, the user, can discover music from multiple sources. By registring through the form below you can search for information about your favorite bands! All you need is a username and a password and you are good to go!

The name Hakkiko is derived from the japanese words "Hakken", which means discover, and "Kikoeru", which means sound. That is really the very essence of Hakkiko, boiled down into a nice acronym! This site exist so you can find more information about the bands you love, and get tips on similar bands and artist which you might enjoy!
