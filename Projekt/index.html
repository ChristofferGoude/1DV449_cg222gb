About

Welcome to Hakkiko! Hakkiko is a website where you, the user, can search for music from multiple sources. The name Hakkiko is derived from the japanese words "Hakken", which means discover, and "Kikoeru", which means sound. That is really the very essence of Hakkiko, boiled down into a nice acronym! This site exist so you can search for music and perhaps discover something new and interesting that you have not heard before.

Start by searching for something in the searchbar above!

Currently Hakkiko is powered by Soundcloud and Deezer, though the goal in the future is to add more sources of music to the mix.
