About

Welcome to Hakkiko! Hakkiko is a website where you, the user, can discover music from multiple sources. By clicking the big button, we we give you a randomized set of tracks to listen to and discover! Don't like what we served? Hit the button again to get some new tunes!

The name Hakkiko is derived from the japanese words "Hakken", which means discover, and "Kikoeru", which means sound. That is really the very essence of Hakkiko, boiled down into a nice acronym! This site exist so you can search for music and perhaps discover something new and interesting that you have not heard before.

Currently Hakkiko is powered by Soundcloud and Deezer, though the goal in the future is to add more sources of music to the mix. All rights and ownership of music shown on this page belongs to the creators shown.
